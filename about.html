Our Story

Where tradition meets innovation in every loaf we bake

Welcome to Artisan Bakery, where tradition meets innovation in every loaf we bake. Founded in 2015 by master baker Ahmed Hassan, our bakery has been serving the Berlin community with authentic breads from around the world.

Our Heritage

Ahmed Hassan brought his family's 200-year-old bread-making traditions from the Middle East to Germany. His grandfather was a renowned baker in Damascus, Syria, where he perfected the art of making traditional Lavash, Barbari, and other regional breads. This legacy continues in our Berlin bakery, where we honor these time-honored techniques while embracing modern quality standards.

Why Choose Artisan Bakery?

Premium Ingredients

We source only the finest organic flour, natural yeast, and traditional spices from trusted suppliers across Europe and the Middle East.

Traditional Methods

Our breads are crafted using traditional techniques including stone-ground flour, natural fermentation, and authentic tandoor ovens.

Fresh Daily

Every morning, our bakers start before dawn to ensure you receive the freshest bread possible, baked to perfection.

Our Commitment to Quality

At Artisan Bakery, we believe that great bread starts with great ingredients and ends with great care. Our commitment to quality extends beyond our products to our service, our community involvement, and our environmental responsibility.

Community & Sustainability

We're proud to be part of the Berlin community and committed to sustainable practices. We use eco-friendly packaging, source ingredients locally when possible, and donate unsold bread to local food banks. Our bakery is more than just a business – it's a gathering place where cultures meet and traditions are shared.
